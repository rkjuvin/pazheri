Pazheri Gold Where tradition is gold and glittering

The story of Pazheri Gold begins with a passion for the creation of finely crafted Jewellery that grew out from a beautiful little town of Mannarkkad in Palakkad district in Kerala state, India. Since 1966, Pazheri Gold has displayed amazing works of art with its insistence on traditional designs combined with modern techniques. The vintage-inspired golden poetry was woven by highly trained traditional gold craftsmen who learned their craft through generations of lineage.
